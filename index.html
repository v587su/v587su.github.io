Introduction

Currently, I'm a PhD student at Singapore Management University supervised by Prof. David Lo, where I'm expected to graduate in August 2026. Prior to joining SMU, I received my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Tongji University. I also gained valuable research experience working as a Research Assistant at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University and ShanghaiTech University.

My research lies at the intersection of software engineering and artificial intelligence. My mission is to engineer a software ecosystem natively designed for both human developers and AI coders, aiming to maximize the productivity of both.

My recent research interests are to address the following two key questions: 1) How to redesign our software artifacts to be AI-friendly? and 2) How to facilitate AI to operate in our ambiguous software development environments?

Feel free to reach out to me if you are also interested in exploring these topics!

🎓 I'm on the job market for Fall 2026.
